Freelance, Web Design



Overview

Through word of mouth, I re- designed and updated a friend's teacher, Nora Woolley's, portfolio site on WordPress. This project lasted from Oct. 2021 to Dec. 2021 and was my first time freelancing with a client before. I met with Nora on a number of consultations to understand what she wanted to keep and throw away, since we working off an already existing outdated WordPress site she had from 2012.


Deliverables

The photos above are comparisons between before (left), after (right), and the T- chart I made to map out simplifying navigation and adding the elements she wanted. And where we originally had 5 tabs, we were able to get her down to two. My priortities were: interactive elements, reduce static text, 100% responsive, and minimalist feel.

Skills: WordPress, Web Design, Consulting, Layout design, Freelance


Takeaways

Biggest takaways were that it was a successful project and I loved getting to work one-on-one with people. I've always wanted to be my own boss but freelancing as an industry is extremely difficult to make a living off of. You must be very careful with everything from time management, clients to billing. Lastly, WordPress itself is outdated and I would not use again with a client. I suggested we make the switch to Squarespace or Wix but since the primary use of her site was for documenting her past work, it made sense not to pay more money for the service.



Personal Branding Challenge



Overview

My interest in Seeking Each Other, or EO for short, was to experiment with branding, social media and communications. I was thinking about re- humanizing our interactions with each other and building a digital and IRL community actively unified in sharing experiences, guidance and information with one another. Although living online it would priortize content that supports the body and healthy media diets (or internet usage).

In a hypercapitalistic society, the general quality of livelihood, solely relys on the individual's conscious. This disporportionality affects commununities in the lower and middler- lower class as there's inequal access to resources and said products, to improve their quality of life. If we funded and emphasized our communal and neighborly relationships, the burden would be much less manageable.

Skills: Branding, UX/ UI, Product/ Web/ Social Media Design, Communications

Takeaways

Since EO's value or product isn't actually a product, I knew I had to emphasize straight- forward and minimalist design communication. I settled with informative and geometric as the best design intention. I learned a lot through this project and how hard it is to start anything from scratch. It was great practice for when I plan to start my own business and apply some of the lessons I learned into ATHT. The full Figma documentation and design documents can be found here: here!
